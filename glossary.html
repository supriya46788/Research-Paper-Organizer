Understand research terms with clear definitions and examples

A B C P R T

Abstract

A brief summary of a research paper or article, highlighting the key points and findings.

Example: The abstract at the start of a scientific paper summarizes the objectives, methods, and results.

Bibliography

A list of the books, articles, and other sources cited or consulted in a research work.

Example: The bibliography section contains all the references used in the paper.

Citation

A reference to a published or unpublished source, used to give credit to the original author.

Example: According to Smith (2020), AI tools are changing research workflows.

Peer Review

The evaluation of scholarly work by experts in the same field to ensure quality and validity. See also: Impact Factor

Example: The paper was accepted after two rounds of peer review.

Research Proposal

A detailed plan outlining the objectives, methodology, and significance of a research project.

Example: A proposal must be approved before starting a funded research project.

Thesis Statement

A single sentence that summarizes the main point or claim of a research paper.

Example: The thesis statement guides the entire structure of the paper.
