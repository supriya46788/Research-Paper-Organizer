About Research Paper Organizer

Organize, Track & Cite Papers Effortlessly

Purpose of the Project

The Research Paper Organizer is a clean, minimal, and static web-based tool built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It helps students, researchers, and professionals neatly organize, categorize, and access their research papers in one place. Our mission is to make research management simple, accessible, and open for everyone.

Founded in 2023, this platform was born out of a simple need: to bring order to the chaos of academic research. Whether you're a PhD student juggling dozens of papers or a researcher managing multiple projects, our tool provides an intuitive interface to track, categorize, and cite your sources efficiently.

Our Vision

We envision a world where researchers can focus on what matters most - their research - without getting bogged down by the administrative overhead of managing countless papers, citations, and references.

Our platform bridges the gap between complex research management tools and simple note-taking applications, providing just the right level of functionality to keep your research organized and accessible.

Key Features

Add and organize research papers with detailed metadata

Categorize papers by topics, projects, or custom tags

Powerful search functionality to find papers quickly

Automatic citation generation in multiple formats

Export functionality for backup and sharing

Clean, responsive design that works on all devices

Dark mode support for comfortable reading

Client-side storage for privacy and security
