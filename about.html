About Us

Who We Are

Research Paper Organizer is a tool designed to help students, researchers, and academics efficiently manage, track, and cite their research papers. Our mission is to simplify the research process and make paper management effortless.

Our Mission

To empower the global research community with a modern, intuitive, and privacy-focused platform for organizing and referencing academic work.

How It Works

  1. Add your research papers with details, tags, and PDF uploads.
  2. Filter, search, and organize your collection by topic, year, or author.
  3. View, edit, or delete papers anytime. Mark favorites for quick access.
  4. Generate citations in APA, MLA, or Chicago style with one click.
