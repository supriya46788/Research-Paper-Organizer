About Us

Organize, Track & Cite Papers Effortlessly

📚 Purpose of the Project

The Research Paper Organizer is a clean, minimal, and static web-based tool built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It helps students, researchers, and professionals neatly organize, categorize, and access their research papers in one place. Our mission is to make research management simple, accessible, and open for everyone.
